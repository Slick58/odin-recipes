Simple Tomato Pasta

This easy tomato pasta is a comforting meal made with pantry staples. The rich tomato sauce coats the pasta perfectly, making it a quick and delicious dish for lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Add minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute.
  4. Pour in the tomato sauce and let it simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Drain the pasta and add it to the sauce.
  6. Toss well to coat the pasta evenly.
  7. Serve with grated parmesan if desired.
