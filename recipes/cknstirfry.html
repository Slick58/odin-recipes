Chicken Stir-Fry

Savory Chicken

Chicken stir-fry is a quick and flavorful dish made with tender chicken pieces and fresh vegetables cooked in a savory sauce. It's perfect for busy weeknights and pairs well with rice or noodles.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts (sliced into thin strips)
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 bell pepper (sliced)
  • 1 carrot (sliced)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce (optional)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch
  • ¼ cup water

Steps

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the sliced chicken and cook until browned and fully cooked.
  3. Remove the chicken from the pan and set aside.
  4. Add garlic and cook for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
  5. Add the broccoli, bell pepper, and carrot to the pan.
  6. Stir-fry the vegetables for 3-4 minutes until slightly tender.
  7. In a small bowl, mix soy sauce, oyster sauce, water, and cornstarch.
  8. Return the chicken to the pan and pour in the sauce mixture.
  9. Stir well and cook until the sauce thickens.
  10. Serve hot over rice or noodles.
